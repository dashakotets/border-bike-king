Bike Repairs You Can Trust

Whether it’s a squeaky brake, a flat tyre, or a complete tune-up, our expert mechanics are here to keep your ride running smoothly. At Borders Bike King, we don’t just fix bikes — we keep your adventures going.

Service Experience

Bring your bike in and we’ll carry out a quick inspection while you talk us through any issues you’ve noticed. After the check, we’ll explain exactly what needs to be done — no jargon, no pressure. You'll receive a clear estimate and timeframe, so you can get back on the road (or trail) with confidence. Turnaround times vary by season, but most repairs are completed within 24–48 hours. We also offer express service for urgent fixes when time is tight.
